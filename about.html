The Story of
Studio NOVA

We didn’t have a plan—just a passion to build. That spark grew into Nova Design, and we’ve never looked back.

How it all began

Over a cup of cofee

It all started with a phone call - and turned into a meeting that changed everything.

For a long time, there was just an idea. Sarindu had always dreamed of building something in the creative tech space - a company that blends vibrant design, clean code, and real-world functionality. But coming from a background in business and medicine, he knew he needed the right team to make it real.

The search took time. What he didn’t realize was that the right people were already close.

Kavisha - a close friend for over a decade - had always been around. But it was through him that Sarindu reconnected with his elder brother, Hirunika - an experienced developer with a sharp eye for design and a strong background in web development. From the very first phone call, it just clicked. The designs, the energy, the vision - it all aligned.

As it turned out, Hirunika had also been carrying the idea of joining a startup in the back of his mind. Having worked with a few before, the concept of building something new wasn’t unfamiliar to him. He loved the direction, saw the potential, and welcomed the idea with open hands. What started as a casual conversation quickly grew into a powerful beginning - full of mutual respect and excitement.
